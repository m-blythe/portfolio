About Me

I'm a DATA ANALYST with 10+ years of experience in interpreting and analyzing data for driving business decisions. I have a knowledge of statistics, analytics and use those skills to help to understand your business processes. Because understanding a business involves using and seeing data I have had to develop the ability to work with numerous sources of data and APIs and then create visualizations that help businesses identify deficiencies in their business operations. I'm not a web developer, however, I use web development to show businesses how they are operating and how they can operate in the future.
